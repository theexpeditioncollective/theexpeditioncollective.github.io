MENTOR

AJAY SOOD

  • He is a Canon Photo Mentor, a Getty Photographer and runs 'Travelure'. He is a journalist member of the Press Club of India.
  • He has captured 47 countries and 102 UNESCO World Heritage Sites.
  • Taken over 100 workshops for Canon, National Geographic, Nikon, Adobe, and more.
  • Has won numerous awards like OSM (Outlook Social Media) Photographer of the Year, Grand winner of National Geographic Traveller's #GetOutThere, Best PhotoBlog Award by Gujarat Tourism and Innovative Council of India Tourism.

What's
Included?

1 / Accommodation
